My Awesome Cart!

My Awesome Cart Pvt Ltd., an innovative Indian e-commerce venture headquartered in Bengaluru, was founded in 2007 by Parth and Nisarg, visionaries committed to reshaping the digital shopping experience. Initially focused on book sales, the company swiftly diversified its offerings to include consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products. A formidable contender in India's e-commerce landscape, My Awesome Cart competes fiercely with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Snapdeal. As of March 2017, the company held an impressive 39.5% market share, showcasing its dominance. Particularly acclaimed for its leadership in apparel sales, bolstered by strategic acquisitions like Myntra and Jabong.com, My Awesome Cart is also recognized for its competitive position in electronics and mobile phone sales, often going neck-and-neck with Amazon. Beyond its core e-commerce operations, My Awesome Cart owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). In a landmark acquisition in August 2018, U.S.-based retail giant Walmart secured a controlling 77% stake in My Awesome Cart for an astounding US$16 billion, valuing the company at an impressive $22 billion, marking a significant milestone in the journey of Parth and Nisarg's visionary enterprise
